Range touch; Targets 1 willing creature

If the target dies of anything other than old age, it can recall its recorded body from the Akashic Record.This process takes 1 minute, after which time its corpse disappears, and the target instantly reappears in the recorded body in the corpse's space and with any worn equipment.
