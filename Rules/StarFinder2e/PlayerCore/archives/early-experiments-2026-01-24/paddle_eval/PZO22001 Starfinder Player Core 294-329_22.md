## ARCTIC RIFT 

SPELL 8

COLD CONCENTRATE MANIPULATE 

Traditions arcane, primal 

Area 120-foot line 

Defense Fortitude 

A jagged crack opens in the air, dealing 12d8 cold damage as it draws away warmth. Each creature along the rift must attempt a Fortitude save.



Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage and is slowed 1until the start of your next turn.



Critical Failure The creature takes double damage, is immobilized by a layer of ice, and is slowed 1 as long as it's immobilized. The ice is an object with 60 Hit Points,Hardness 5, immunity to cold damage, and vulnerability 10to fire. It has object immunities (page 236) and is destroyed if the target Escapes.



Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d8 and the ice's Hit Points increase by 5.
